Endangered Fish in North Carolina

There are three fish listed as endangered by N.C. Wildlife. these are the Cape Fear Shiner, the Roanoke Hogsucker, and the Robust Redhorse. There are also four fish species listed as threatened which include the Maccamaw Silverside, the Spotfin Chub, the Sicklefin Redhorse, and the Carolina Redhorse.
